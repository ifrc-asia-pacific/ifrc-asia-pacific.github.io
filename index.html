    Tools

    IFRC GO Platform

    The IFRC GO Platform is the latest developed web-based portal with all disaster information universally accessible and useful to IFRC responders for better decision making.

    FDRS

    The IFRC GO Platform is the latest developed web-based portal with all disaster information universally accessible and useful to IFRC responders for better decision making.

    RMS

    Resources Management System (RMS) enables National Societies to view and analyse hazards and vulnerabilities together with their data on human resources such as volunteers, staff, offices and warehouses.

    Humanitarian ID

    Humanitarian ID is a contact management solution manage by OCHA for use in humanitarian situations.

    Humanitarian Data Exchange (HDX)

    The Humanitarian Data Exchange (HDX) aims to accelerate data flows in the humanitarian sector and reduce the time between data collection and decisions.

    Humanitarian Exchange Language (HXL)

    HXL is a different kind of data standard, designed to improve information sharing during a humanitarian crisis without adding extra reporting burdens.

      Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Team (HOT)

      The Humanitarian OpenStreetMap Team (HOT) provides free, up-to-date maps for responding to disasters or crises.

    The International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC) is the world's largest humanitarian organization, with 190 member National Societies. As part of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement, our work is guided by seven fundamental principles; humanity, impartiality, neutrality, independence, voluntary service, unity and universality.
